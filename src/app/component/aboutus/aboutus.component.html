Our History

The bank has been formed from the merger and acquisition of nearly twenty banks over the course of its 200 year history.The Government of India took control of the Imperial Bank of India in 1955,renaming it State Bank of India.

Who we are!

State Bank of India (SBI) is an Indian multinational public sector bank and financial services statutory body headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra.
